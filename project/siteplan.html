Siza Creations

Website Planning Document

Site Name

Siza Creations

I chose the name "Siza Creations" because it ties directly to my identity as an artist and animator. "Siza" comes from my surname, Msiza, and connects to my YouTube channel Caesar_Siza where I have been posting story time animations since 2016. The word "Creations" reflects the range of work I do — from animated storytelling to character design and graphic art. It is simple, personal, and broad enough to grow with me as my creative work evolves.

Optional domain: sizacreations.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of Siza Creations is to serve as a professional portfolio showcasing my journey as a story time animator and character artist. The site will feature animated projects from my YouTube channel Caesar_Siza, including work like "Third wheel/candleholder" and "Anxiety," along with character designs, graphic design studies, and newer projects from after my mission. Visitors will be able to explore my work chronologically, watch embedded animations, view character art galleries, and understand how my style has developed over the years. The site gives me a dedicated space to present my creative work outside of YouTube in a way that I fully control.

Scenarios

  • I saw one of your animations on YouTube and want to see more of your work — where can I browse all of your animation projects in one place?
  • I am interested in your character design style — can I see examples of your character art and learn about your creative process?
  • How has your animation and art style changed since you started in 2016?

Color Schema

The color palette is designed to reflect a creative, bold animation portfolio. The dark background creates a cinematic feel that lets artwork stand out, while the vibrant orange and magenta accents bring energy and personality.

#0f0e17 Page background, footer
#1c1b29 Card and section backgrounds
#ff8906 Headings, buttons, accents
#e53170 Links, highlights, decorative borders
#a7a9be Body and paragraph text

Typography

Bebas Neue

This Is Bebas Neue for Headings

Used for page headings, section titles, and hero text. Bebas Neue is bold and cinematic — it gives the site a strong visual identity that fits an animation portfolio.

Poppins

This is Poppins for body text. It is clean, modern, and easy to read at any size. The geometric shapes give it a contemporary feel without sacrificing readability.

Used for paragraph text, navigation, labels, and all general content across the site.
